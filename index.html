Hello!

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm a student at the Lemur University of Rhode Island currently studying User Interfaces and User Experience. I'm interested in the intersection of design and technology, and my core design principles are simplicity, accessibility, and inclusivity.

Please feel free to browse my work below!

Projects

Personas and Storyboarding

In this project, I construct two personas of users of my dishwasher with corresponding empathy maps based on data collected from multiple observation sessions in my apartment's kitchen in Providence. I also present a storyboard depicting the process by which a certain user persona might use my dishwasher.

Responsive Redesign

In this project, I practiced the workflow of redesigning a simple website. I exercised my skills in analyzing and identifying flaws in an existing interface, creating low-fidelity and high-fidelity prototypes for various screen sizes, and building a responsive website based on those prototypes.

Iterative Design and Evaluation

In this project, I worked with in a team of four to design a website for a real-world startup that provides a platform for helping workers unionize their workplace. I practiced the workflow of iterative design and evaluation by creating low-fidelity and high-fidelity prototypes, conducting usability tests, and iterating on the design based on the results of the tests.
